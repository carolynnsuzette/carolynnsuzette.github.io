Receptions

Specialized Features:

  • Customizable Arrangements
  • Food Service
  • Kitchen Access
  • Setup

Customizable Arrangements

Temple Inn & Suites offers a wedding reception hall that has various arrangment options. The reception hall is customizable for the needs of those who use it. This includes customizable food menu options and setup arrangements.

Food Service

We offer several simple catering menus for food service. This provides an entree, side, and dessert. Meals can either be served by staff or buffet style. We provide a different variety of food options such as Chinese, Mexican, Italian, and American dishes. There are also a variety of side and dessert options.

Kitchen Access

The kitchen is attached to the reception hall and is open for those helping with the wedding reception. It has large countertop spaces, multiple sinks with running water, a freezer, a refrigerator, and a limited amount of cooking utensils and dishes.

Setup

The setup services include tables for guests, chairs, large rectangular tables for buffet-style food setup, white table cloths, and a simple selection of additional decorations such as centerpieces. Staff will assist in setup.
